What is UX? And what is CPHUX?

UX is short for user experience. It's an umbrella term that covers around 13 design specialities. There is NO industri acknowledge definition of UX. UX is traditionally the overlap between, business, tech, and the user.

At CPHUX, UX is more focused on the fact that our designers want to create the best experiences for the users. We don't care if a designer identify as a Digital Designer, UX'er, CX'er, Interaction Designer, Service Designer, Innovation Consultant etc.

A users experience is at the core of most design and because we're focused on building the community here in Copenhagen we're called CPHUX.
